🎰 How to Play Roulette

📋 Game Overview

Roulette is a classic casino game where you bet on where a ball will land on a spinning wheel. The wheel has numbers 0-36, with numbers colored red or black (0 is green).

🎯 How to Play

  • Select Bet Amount: Choose how much you want to bet using the chip buttons or enter a custom amount
  • Place Your Bets: Click on any bet type to place that bet. You can place multiple different bets!
  • Spin the Wheel: Click "SPIN!" to start the round
  • Watch & Win: The wheel spins and if your bet wins, you get paid immediately!

💰 Bet Types & Payouts

Single Number (0-36) 36x
Column Bet (12 numbers) 3x
Dozen Bet (12 numbers) 3x
Red or Black 2x
Even or Odd 2x
Low (1-18) or High (19-36) 2x

📊 Bet Explanations

Red/Black: Bet on the color of the winning number. 0 is neither red nor black.

Even/Odd: Bet on whether the number is even or odd. 0 doesn't count as even.

Low/High: Low = numbers 1-18, High = numbers 19-36.

Columns: Three vertical columns on the betting table. Column 1 includes 1,4,7,10... Column 2 includes 2,5,8,11... Column 3 includes 3,6,9,12...

Dozens: 1st dozen = 1-12, 2nd dozen = 13-24, 3rd dozen = 25-36.

Single Number: Bet on one specific number for the highest payout!

💡 Tips

  • You can place multiple bets on the same spin
  • Higher payouts mean lower probability of winning
  • The 0 (green) gives the house its edge
  • Manage your bankroll - don't bet more than you can afford to lose!

🎲 Good Luck!

Remember, roulette is a game of chance. Play responsibly and have fun!
